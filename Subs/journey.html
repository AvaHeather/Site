Journey ...

February 12th 2018 ...

So today i got my fingerprints taken to get confirmation of identity, in my name changing process .

Boarding the first bus to my destination felt surreal to what i was to do today. My mind couldn't yet register it, but it dawned on me as the bus speed away..
"This is really happening!!" (something that seemed like a long shot for me)
Having hit dead ends in hope of living, today was God-sent in my life and i can't be more grateful far beyond what i feel now. Sitting in my bed of about 2years where i find solace when the world is tough on me, i am in and out of tears looking at how far i've come through this journey.

I am really proud of my Faith, Friends i've called family and the love & support they gave me and the sparks of hope that lit in my closet because i have never been as confident of myself as i am now, living as me finally without chains and closets holding me back. I owe so much appreciation to all the people that added substance to my life and how far it makes me sore with every little progress i make. To the doubt, the voices, faces and fear that clipped my wings when i tried to fly out of the closet, ....SEE ME Now! cause i am proud to see myself now from where i stand.
